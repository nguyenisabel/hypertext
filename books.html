Some of My Favorite Books

My favorite book, Normal People, is primarily a character study. This aligns a lot with my psychology major, as the book examines two people and their relationship spanning over eight years.

  1. Normal People by Sally Rooney
  2. Daisy Jones and the Six by Taylor Jenkins Reid
  3. The Song of Achilles by Madeline Miller
  4. The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo by Taylor Jenkins Reid
  5. On Earth We're Briefly Gorgeous by Ocean Vuong
  6. Station Eleven by Emily St. John Mandel
